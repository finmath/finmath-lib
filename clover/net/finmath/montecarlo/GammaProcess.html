20    * a Gamma process and \( \Gamma_{i} \), \( \Gamma_{j} \) are
21    * independent for <i>i</i> not equal <i>j</i>.
22    *
23    * The increments \( \Delta \Gamma \) are Gamma distributed with shape parameter <code>shape * (t-s)</code>
24    * for \( t-s = \Delta t \) and scale parameter 1.
25    *
26    * Here the dimension <i>n</i> is called factors since this Gamma process is used to

30    *
31    * The quintruppel (time discretization, number of factors, number of paths, seed, shape)
