13    /**
14    * Models the notional dependent survival probability and default compensation
15    * of a funding capacity (funding provider)
16    * using a piecewise constant function for the instantaneous survival probability.
17    *
18    * The piecewise constant instantaneous survival probability has to be provided
19    * by a SortedMap&lt;Double, Double&gt; instantaneouseSurvivalProbability.
20    *
21    * This map defines the mapping \( x_{i} \mapsto q_{i} \). Defining
22    * \[ q(x) = q_{i} \text{\ for\ } x \in (x_{i-1}-x_{i}] \] the
23    * <code>getDefaultFactors</code> method of this class calculates
24    * for a given argument \( (t,x) \):
25    * <dl>
26    * <dt>
27    * the effective survival probability
28    * </dt>
29    * <dd>
30    * \[ \frac{1}{x} \int_{a}^{a+x} q(\xi) \mathrm{d}\xi \],
31    *
32    * where a denotes the current level of fund provided by this capacity, and
33    * </dd>
34    * <dt>
35    * the effective default compensation factor R, such that
36    * </dt>
37    * <dd>
38    * \[ \frac{1}{x} \int_{a}^{a+R x} q(\xi) \mathrm{d}\xi \ = \ 1 \],
39    * </dd>
40    * </dl>
41    *
42    * Important: since the class keeps track of past fundings
43    * used, it is mandatory that the factors are calculated in
44    * time-sequential order.
45    *
46    * @author Christian Fries
47    */
