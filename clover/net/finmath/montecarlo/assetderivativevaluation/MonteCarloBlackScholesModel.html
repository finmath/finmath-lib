20    /**
21    * This class glues together a <code>BlackScholeModel</code> and a Monte-Carlo implementation of a <code>MonteCarloProcessFromProcessModel</code>
22    * and forms a Monte-Carlo implementation of the Black-Scholes Model by implementing <code>AssetModelMonteCarloSimulationModel</code>.
23    *
24    * The model is
25    * \[
26    * dS = r S dt + \sigma S dW, \quad S(0) = S_{0},
27    * \]
28    * \[
29    * dN = r N dt, \quad N(0) = N_{0},
30    * \]
31    *
32    * The class provides the model of S to an <code>{@link net.finmath.montecarlo.process.MonteCarloProcess}</code> via the specification of
33    * \( f = exp \), \( \mu = r - \frac{1}{2} \sigma^2 \), \( \lambda_{1,1} = \sigma \), i.e.,
34    * of the SDE
35    * \[
36    * dX = \mu dt + \lambda_{1,1} dW, \quad X(0) = \log(S_{0}),
37    * \]
38    * with \( S = f(X) \). See {@link net.finmath.montecarlo.process.MonteCarloProcess} for the notation.
39    *
40    * @author Christian Fries
