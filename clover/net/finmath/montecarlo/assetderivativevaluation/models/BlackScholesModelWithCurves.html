16    /**
17    * This class implements a Black Scholes Model, that is, it provides the drift and volatility specification
18    * and performs the calculation of the numeraire (consistent with the dynamics, i.e. the drift).
19    *
20    * The model is
21    * \[
22    * dS = r S dt + \sigma S dW, \quad S(0) = S_{0},
23    * \]
24    * \[
25    * dN = r N dt, \quad N(0) = N_{0},
26    * \]
27    *
28    * The class provides the model of S to an <code>{@link net.finmath.montecarlo.process.MonteCarloProcess}</code> via the specification of
29    * \( f = exp \), \( \mu = r - \frac{1}{2} \sigma^2 \), \( \lambda_{1,1} = \sigma \), i.e.,
30    * of the SDE
31    * \[
32    * dX = \mu dt + \lambda_{1,1} dW, \quad X(0) = \log(S_{0}),
33    * \]
34    * with \( S = f(X) \). See {@link net.finmath.montecarlo.process.MonteCarloProcess} for the notation.
35    *
36    * @author Christian Fries
