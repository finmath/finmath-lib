10    /**
11    * Implements the characteristic function of a Merton jump diffusion model.
12    *
13    * The model is
14    * \[
15    * dS = \mu S dt + \sigma S dW + S dJ, \quad S(0) = S_{0},
16    * \]
17    * \[
18    * dN = r N dt, \quad N(0) = N_{0},
19    * \]
20    * where \( W \) is Brownian motion and \( J \) is a jump process (compound Poisson process).
21    *
22    * The process \( J \) is given by \( J(t) = \sum_{i=1}^{N(t)} (Y_{i}-1) \), where
23    * \( \log(Y_{i}) \) are i.i.d. normals with mean \( a - \frac{1}{2} b^{2} \) and standard deviation \( b \).
24    * Here \( a \) is the jump size mean and \( b \) is the jump size std. dev.
