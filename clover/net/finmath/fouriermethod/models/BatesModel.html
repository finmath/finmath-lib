16    /**
17    * Implements the characteristic function of a Bates model.
18    *
19    * The Bates model for an underlying \( S \) is given by
20    * \[
21    * dS(t) = r^{\text{c}} S(t) dt + \sqrt{V(t)} S(t) dW_{1}(t) + S dJ, \quad S(0) = S_{0},
22    * \]
23    * \[
24    * dV(t) = \kappa ( \theta - V(t) ) dt + \xi \sqrt{V(t)} dW_{2}(t), \quad V(0) = \sigma^2,
25    * \]
26    * \[
27    * dW_{1} dW_{2} = \rho dt
28    * \]
29    * \[
30    * dN(t) = r^{\text{d}} N(t) dt, \quad N(0) = N_{0},
31    * \]
32    * where \( W \) is Brownian motion and \( J \) is a jump process (compound Poisson process).
33    *
34    * The free parameters of this model are:
35    * <dl>
36    * <dt>\( S_{0} \)</dt> <dd>spot - initial value of S</dd>
37    * <dt>\( r \)</dt> <dd>the risk free rate</dd>
38    * <dt>\( \sigma \)</dt> <dd>the initial volatility level</dd>
39    * <dt>\( \xi \)</dt> <dd>the volatility of volatility</dd>
40    * <dt>\( \theta \)</dt> <dd>the mean reversion level of the stochastic volatility</dd>
41    * <dt>\( \kappa \)</dt> <dd>the mean reversion speed of the stochastic volatility</dd>
42    * <dt>\( \rho \)</dt> <dd>the correlation of the Brownian drivers</dd>
43    * <dt>\( a \)</dt> <dd>the jump size mean</dd>
44    * <dt>\( b \)</dt> <dd>the jump size standard deviation</dd>
45    * </dl>
46    *
47    * The process \( J \) is given by \( J(t) = \sum_{i=1}^{N(t)} (Y_{i}-1) \), where
48    * \( \log(Y_{i}) \) are i.i.d. normals with mean \( a - \frac{1}{2} b^{2} \) and standard deviation \( b \).
49    * Here \( a \) is the jump size mean and \( b \) is the jump size std. dev.
50    *
51    * The model can be rewritten as \( S = \exp(X) \), where
52    * \[
53    * dX = \mu dt + \sqrt{V(t)} dW + dJ^{X}, \quad X(0) = \log(S_{0}),
54    * \]
55    * with
56    * \[
57    * J^{X}(t) = \sum_{i=1}^{N(t)} \log(Y_{i})
58    * \]
59    * with \( \mu = r - \frac{1}{2} \sigma^2 - (exp(a)-1) \lambda \).
60    *
61    *
62    * @author Christian Fries
63    * @author Andy Graf
64    * @author Lorenzo Toricelli
65    * @version 1.0
66    */
