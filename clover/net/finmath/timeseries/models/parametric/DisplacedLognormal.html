20    /**
21    * Displaced log-normal process with constanst volatility.
22    *
23    * This class estimate the process
24    * \[
25    * \mathrm{d} \log(X + a) = \frac{\sigma}{b + a} \mathrm{d}W(t)
26    * \]
27    * where \( a &gt; -min(X(t_{i}) \) and thus \( X+a &gt; 0 \) and \( b = 1 - -min(X(t_{i}) \) \) and
28    * \( \sigma \) is a constant.
29    *
30    * The choice of b ensures that b+a &ge; 1.
31    * For a=0 we have a log-normal process with volatility &sigma;/(b + a).
32    * For a=infinity we have a normal process with volatility &sigma;.

34    * @author Christian Fries
