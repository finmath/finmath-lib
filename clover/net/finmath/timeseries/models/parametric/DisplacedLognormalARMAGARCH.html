26    /**
27    * Displaced log-normal process with ARMAGARCH(1,1) volatility.
28    *
29    * This class estimate the process
30    * \[
31    * \mathrm{d} \log(X + a) = \frac{\sigma}{b + a} \mathrm{d}W(t)
32    * \]
33    * where \( a &gt; -min(X(t_{i}) \) and thus \( X+a &gt; 0 \) and \( b = 1 - -min(X(t_{i}) \) \) and
34    * \( \sigma \) is given by a ARMAGARCH(1,1) process.
35    *
36    * The choice of b ensures that b+a &ge; 1.
37    * For a=0 we have a log-normal process with volatility &sigma;/(b + a).
38    * For a=infinity we have a normal process with volatility &sigma;.
39    *
40    * @author Christian Fries
