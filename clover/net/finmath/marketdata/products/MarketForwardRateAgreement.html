12    /**
13    * Implements the valuation of a market forward rate agreement using curves
14    * (discount curve, forward curve).
15    *
16    * The value of the forward rate agreement at its maturity time <i>t</i> is
17    * <br>
18    * <i>
19    * (F(t)-K) / (1 + F(t) * dcf(periodStart,periodEnd))
20    * </i>
21    * where <i>F(t)</i> is the forward evaluated at maturity and
22    * dcf(periodStart,periodEnd) is a given paymentOffset.
23    *
24    * The value of the forward rate agreement returned for an earlier time is
25    * the above payoff multiplied with the corresponding discount factor curve.
26    * Note that this valuation ignores a possible convexity adjustment between
27    * the forward and the discount factors since the above formula is not a
28    * linear function of <i>F</i>. Put differently, if this product is used
29    * to calibrate a forward curve to a forward rate agreement, then the
30    * calibrated forward curve will include the convexity adjustment.
31    *
32    * @author Christian Fries
33    * @version 1.0
34    */
