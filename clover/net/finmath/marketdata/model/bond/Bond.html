23    /**
24    * Implements the valuation of a bond (zero-coupon, fixed coupon or floating coupon)
25    * with unit notional of 1 using curves:
26    * <ul>
27    * <li>a forward curve, if the bond has floating rate coupons</li>
28    * <li>a discount curve as a base curve for discounting</li>
29    * <li>a survival probability curve for additional credit risk related discount factor</li>
30    * <li>a basis factor curve for additional bond related discount factor</li>
31    * </ul>
32    *
33    * Support for day counting is provided via the class implementing
34    * <code>Schedule</code>.
35    *
36    * The effective bond curve is a combination of the discount curve (risk free curve), the basis factor curve
37    * (which could be considers as an additional industry specific factor) and the issuer specific
38    * survival probalbilty. The effective discount factor is the product of the three:
39    * discountFactor * survivalProbabilityFactor * basisFactorFactor
40    *
41    * You may set the arguments for the survival probability curve and the basis factor curve to null.
42    *
43    * @author Moritz Scherrmann
44    * @author Chrisitan Fries
45    * @version 1.1
46    */
