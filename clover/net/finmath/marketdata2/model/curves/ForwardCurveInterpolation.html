24    /**
25    * A container for a forward (rate) curve. The forward curve is based on the {@link net.finmath.marketdata.model.curves.CurveInterpolation} class.
26    * It thus features all interpolation and extrapolation methods and interpolation entities as {@link net.finmath.marketdata.model.curves.CurveInterpolation}.
27    *
28    * The forward F(t) of an index is such that * F(t) * D(t+p) equals the market price of the corresponding
29    * index fixed in t and paid in t+d, where t is the fixing time of the index and t+p is the payment time of the index.
30    * F(t) is the corresponding forward and D is the associated discount curve.
31    *
32    * @author Christian Fries
33    * @version 1.0
34    */
